4. ATTENTION IN LLM

The attention mechanism is one of the most important concepts in LLM , it allows the model to focus on the most relevant words (tokens) when processing or generating text , instead of treating every word equally.

Attention = Important Sentence

To handle attention mechanism every model has 3 vectors:-
Query(Q) - What info am I looking for?
Key(K) - What information do I contain?
Value(V) - What information should I contribute ?


Single words == Multiple Meanings (single self attention mechanism)
Multiple word == Multiple meaning ( Multi head attention)
